**Prevention Checklist**



Stay Safe Online :-

\-> Do not make any payment request unless you are well aware of the person who sent the request.

\-> Always check the name and details twice before commencing any transaction.

\-> Do not give out your OTP, PIN or banking details to any other person, even if that person purports to be a bank.

\-> Never click on an unrelated link in a message or email.

\-> Attempt to use known and official applications to make payments over the Internet.

\-> Always upgrade your phone and applications to prevent security-related threats.

\-> Watch out when transacting money over the open Wi-Fi.

\-> When something smells fishy, then it is best to pause and confirm.



UPI Safety (Important) :-

\-> It is important to remember that with a UPI request, money will not be received but will be debited.

\-> Confirming always requires a careful reading of the payment message.

\-> Do not scan QR codes that have been provided by unknown or unverified sources.



In Case of Fraud :-

\-> Report the case by submitting it as soon as possible on the cybercrime.gov.in.

\-> Preferably dial the national cybercrime helpline 1930.

\-> Report to your bank as fast as possible and avoid additional loss.
